Determination of liquid density using Archimedes' law

Objective of the exercise: Determining the density of an unknown liquid based on the motion of a free-falling body through that medium.
Description of the exercise:
A ball of known mass m and 2 bottles are given in the apparatus of the exercise, one of which contains water of known density p_0, and the other contains liquid of unknown density p_t.
Based on the measurement of the time of free fall in water, determine the volume of the solid body, and then determine the density of the unknown liquid when measuring the time of free fall in it.

Ignore the influence of the resistance force of the environment.
